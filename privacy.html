Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 26, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Kamarilla Stats ("we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect

Kamarilla Stats is a personal analytics tool. We collect only the data necessary to provide social media statistics, including:

  • Public social media account data (video views, likes, comments, shares) accessed via official platform APIs
  • Authentication tokens required to access platform APIs on your behalf

2. How We Use Information

The information collected is used solely to:

  • Retrieve and display statistics from your connected social media accounts
  • Send periodic analytics reports to your configured Telegram account

3. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your own device or server. We do not operate any central servers or databases. No personal data is transmitted to third parties.

4. Third-Party Platforms

Our application connects to the following platforms via their official APIs:

  • TikTok — TikTok Privacy Policy
  • YouTube — Google Privacy Policy
  • Instagram — Instagram Privacy Policy

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your data with any third parties.

6. Data Retention

Statistics history is stored locally in JSON files on your device. You may delete these files at any time.
